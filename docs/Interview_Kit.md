# Cybersecurity Interview Prep Kit

## Common Questions
1.  **Explain the difference between encoding, encryption, and hashing.**
2.  **How would you investigate a suspicious PowerShell script?**
3.  **Describe the 3-way handshake.**
4.  **What is the difference between a false positive and a false negative?**

## STAR Method
*   **Situation**: Describe the context.
*   **Task**: What was your goal?
*   **Action**: What did you do?
*   **Result**: What was the outcome?
